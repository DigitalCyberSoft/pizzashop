   _layout:_ Around the ring: two adjacent tomato slices with pepperoni+olive, two adjacent cheese slices with mushroom+sweetcorn, repeat once.  
   _concept:_ diagonal quarters

   _layout:_ One adjacent pair is bbq only; the other six bbq slices have chicken+onion+one green topping such as spinach, broccoli, peas, green beans, pepper, or olive.  
   _concept:_ everything except one quarter with colour grouping

   _layout:_ Any mix of tomato and cheese bases is acceptable if all tomato-base slices have bacon and no ham, and all cheese-base slices have pineapple+raisins.  
   _concept:_ self-correction
4. "On every cheese slice, give me any three different vegetables; on every bbq slice, give me more than two meats."  
   _layout:_ Cheese-base slices each have exactly any 3 different vegetables; bbq-base slices each have at least 3 meat toppings.  
   _concept:_ any N different category plus count comparison

   _layout:_ Four consecutive tomato slices each have 0-4 toppings; the other four consecutive bbq slices each have at least 3 meat toppings.  
   _concept:_ count comparisons by half

   _layout:_ In cyclic order: 3 tomato slices each with any 2 different fruits; then 3 cheese slices each with any 2 different silly toppings; then 2 bbq slices each with at least 3 meats.
