Sound effects
=============

All sound effects in this folder are from Kenney (www.kenney.nl) and are released
under the Creative Commons Zero (CC0 1.0 Universal) public-domain dedication. No
attribution is required; this file is provided as a courtesy and a record.

Kenney packs (all CC0, from https://kenney.nl):
  - Interface Sounds   -> pick, place, erase, box, meh, fail, tipLost
  - Music Jingles      -> success, perfect, fanfare, legend, sixseven
  - Casino Audio       -> coin

cheer.mp3 -> a real crowd applause recording, CC0:
  "applause-2" by sandermotions (Freesound #277021), via Wikimedia Commons
  https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:277021_sandermotions_applause-2.wav
  Trimmed to ~2.6s, faded, loudness-normalized, converted to mono mp3.

The .ogg/.wav originals were converted to mono mp3 (iOS Safari cannot play .ogg).
Regenerate or re-map in tools/ if you change the selection.
